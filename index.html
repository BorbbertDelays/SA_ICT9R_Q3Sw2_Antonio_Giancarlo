Minions - The Rise of Gru
Evolving from single-celled yellow organisms at the dawn of time, Minions live to serve, but find themselves working for a continual series of unsuccessful masters, from T. Rex to Napoleon. Without a master to grovel for, the Minions fall into a deep depression. But one minion, Kevin, has a plan; accompanied by his pals Stuart and Bob, Kevin sets forth to find a new evil boss for his brethren to follow. Their search leads them to Scarlet Overkill, the world's first-ever super-villainess. Cast:
Sandra Bullock, Pierre Coffin, Steve Coogan, Michael Keaton

Director:
Steven Knight

Runtime: 91 minutes 		FeastMode:

The story is surprisingly unpredictable and keeps you invested. The visuals are stunningly beautiful. I was mesmerized. And the action scenes are pretty epic, the best of the entire series."


Code 8
A desperate young man with special powers commits a series of petty crimes and is hunted by a militarised police force. Cast:
Robbie Amell, Stephen Amell, Sung Kang, Kari Matchett

Director:
Jeff Chan

Runtime: 91 minutes 		Hughmanity:
"Worth watching for a decent mashup of Sci-Fi and Heist genres. Some all-too-convenient plot devices abound along with the now-familiar premise of powered people being discriminated against. It had a chance to be very good and instead was just decent, but I'll watch a decent Sci-Fi flick any time."


Mea Culpa
A criminal defense attorney takes on the case of a seductive artist accused of murdering his girlfriend, but when burning desire takes hold, things get hot -- and dangerous. Cast:
Kelly Rowland, Trevante Rhodes, Nick Sagar

Director:
